# Code Of Conduct

Timet is a collaborative creative-and-technical project. We want the community around it to be respectful, thoughtful, and grounded.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and patient.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Prefer clear, evidence-based discussion.
- Welcome contributors across languages, backgrounds, and skill levels.
- Avoid harassment, intimidation, or hostile behavior.

## Project Expectations

- Do not present fabricated claims as verified history.
- Do not turn fictional or historical strategy content into real-world harm advocacy.
- Do not introduce fake AI behavior or knowingly misleading product claims.
- Be careful with any changes that affect safety boundaries, permissions, or trust signals.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a private moderator contact through GitHub if possible. For security concerns, use the path in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
